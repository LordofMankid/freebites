# Freebites Community Guidelines

Last updated: 5/13/2024

## The Short

Freebites is a platform for sharing food opportunities at Tufts while maintaining a respectful environment. Post accurate food details, avoid spamming or impersonation, and respect others' rights and privacy. Don't engage in illegal activities, and report any violations to help keep the community safe.

## The Long

Freebites is a reflection of our diverse community of cultures, ages, and beliefs. We've spent a lot of time thinking about the different points of view that create a safe and open environment for everyone.

We created the Community Guidelines so you can help us foster and protect this amazing community. By using Freebites, you agree to these guidelines. We're committed to these guidelines and we hope you are too. Overstepping these boundaries may result in deleted content, disabled accounts, or other restrictions.

### Be thoughtful when posting food opportunities.

We recognize that Freebites is a platform where users come together to share valuable food-related information. While we encourage this exchange, it's important to be mindful of the content you share, especially considering the diverse audience that uses our platform.

When posting food opportunities, ensure that the photos accurately represent the items available and include relevant information such as descriptions, filters, location details, and the current status of the food (through the live thread). Deceptive practices or misleading information can harm the trust within our community and may lead to the removal of such content and account. There's no tolerance for objectionable content or abusive users. Users can flag objectionable content and block abusive users if necessary.

### Foster meaningful and genuine interactions.

Help us stay [spam-free](https://www.facebook.com/communitystandards/spam) by not artificially collecting comments and attention or posting repetitive comments or content. You don't have to use your real name on Freebites, but we do require Freebites users to provide us with accurate anulad up to date information. Don't [impersonate](https://www.facebook.com/communitystandards/misrepresentation) others and don't create accounts for the purpose of violating our guidelines or [misleading others](https://transparency.fb.com/policies/community-standards/inauthentic-behavior/).

### Follow the law.

Freebites is dedicated to promoting a safe and positive environment for sharing food opportunities within the Tufts community. Therefore, we strictly prohibit any form of malicious commentary, including support or praise for [terrorism, organized crime, or hate groups.](https://www.facebook.com/communitystandards/dangerous_individuals_organizations) Additionally, content related to offering sexual services, offering firearms, alcohol, and tobacco products among individuals, as well as the postings of non-medical or pharmaceutical drugs, is strictly prohibited.

We do not tolerate any attempts to coordinate the trade or distribution of non-medical drugs, nor do we allow content that promotes their use. Furthermore, the offerings of live animals between private individuals is not permitted on Freebites.

Always ensure that any content you post on Freebites complies with the law. Avoid sharing inaccurate or unreliable food availability, as this undermines the trust and integrity of our community. We are committed to maintaining a zero-tolerance policy

regarding the sharing of sexual content or threats to post [intimate images](https://www.facebook.com/communitystandards/sexual_exploitation_adults) of others.

### Share only photos and videos that you've taken or have the right to share.

As always, you own the content you post on Freebites. Remember to post authentic content, and don't post anything you've copied or collected from the Internet that you don't have the right to post. Learn more about [intellectual property rights](https://stfrancislaw.com/blog/intellectual-property-rights/#:~:text=There%20are%20four%20main%20types,protect%20the%20same%20intangible%20assets.).

### Respect other members of the Freebites community.

We want to foster a positive, diverse community. We remove content that contains [credible threats](https://www.facebook.com/communitystandards/credible_violence) or [hate speech](https://www.facebook.com/communitystandards/hate_speech), content that targets private individuals to [degrade or shame them](https://www.facebook.com/communitystandards/bullying), personal information meant to blackmail or harass someone, and repeated unwanted messages.  
It's never OK to encourage violence or attack anyone based on their race, ethnicity, national origin, sex, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, religious affiliation, disabilities, or diseases.

[Serious threats of harm](https://www.facebook.com/communitystandards/credible_violence) to public and personal safety aren't allowed. This includes specific threats of physical harm as well as threats of theft, vandalism, and other financial harm. We carefully review reports of threats and consider many things when determining whether a threat is credible.

### Help us keep the community strong.

Each of us is an important part of the Freebites community. If you see something that you think may violate our guidelines, please help us by using our built-in reporting option. We have a team that reviews these reports and works as quickly as possible to remove content that doesn't meet our guidelines. When you complete the report, try to provide as much information as possible, such as the specific elements of the post that generated discomfort and your rationales, so we can review it quickly. We may remove entire posts if either the imagery or associated captions violate our guidelines.

We may work with law enforcement, including when we believe that there's risk of physical harm or threat to public safety.

Thank you for helping us create one of the best communities in the world.

The Freebites Team
